Project Overview

Manage your finances effortlessly with Bankist, a modern online banking application designed with a minimalist aesthetic for a clean and intuitive user experience. Bankist offers a smooth and user-friendly interface, allowing you to easily track your income and expenses, transfer funds, and access other essential banking functionalities - all from the comfort of your device.

To gather inspiration for a compelling user interface, I actively researched websites within the industry and explored platforms like Dribbble.
